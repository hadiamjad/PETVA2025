PET-VA2025

2025 Workshop on Privacy-Enhancing Technologies (PET-VA)

Dates: Thursday, May 8, 2025
Time: 11:00 AM - 03:00 PM
Location: Virginia Tech, Blacksburg (Torgersen Hall - Room 1100)

This workshop is designed to unite researchers from the Commonwealth of Virginia who are interested in exploring both the privacy implications of online applications and the development and implementation of privacy-enhancing technologies (PET) for diverse user segments. By showcasing past work and fostering discussions on future directions, the event provides a unique forum for building new interdisciplinary partnerships and sparking innovative collaborations. The workshop will cover topics ranging from emerging methods to privacy-enhancing technologies to large-scale privacy and security measurements. This event is open to all researchers and students within the CCI network as well as the broader privacy and security research community. We will bring together experts from diverse disciplines to explore interdisciplinary approaches to PIA research. Key topics include:

  • Privacy Implications in Online Applications (covering websites, mobile apps, smart devices, AR/VR, etc.)
  • Privacy Implications for Users with Disabilities
  • Large-scale Measurements for Security and Privacy
  • AI Language Technologies For Transparent Human-Computer Interactions

We look forward to your participation and hope you will join us in shaping the future of privacy-enhancing technologies and measurement methods.
